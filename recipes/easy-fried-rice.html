Easy Vegetable & Egg Fried Rice

Return to Home

Description

The ultimate comfort food: A steaming, homemade lasagna, fresh out of the oven. It’s baked to a perfect golden-brown and fills the room with the mouth-watering scent of savory bolognese and herbs. That magical moment when the melted mozzarella stretches into gooey strings promises pure coziness. Garnished with fresh basil—a slice of Italian warmth on a plate.

Ingredients

The Base

  • Cooked Rice: 2–3 cups of cold, day-old rice
  • Oil: Vegetable or canola oil

The Mix-ins

  • Eggs: 2–3 large eggs, whisked.
  • Vegetables: 1 cup mixed veggies (peas, carrots, corn – frozen mix is fine).
  • Aromatics: 2 cloves garlic (minced), 1 small onion or shallot (diced).
  • Green Onions: Chopped (separate white and green parts).

The Sauce

  • Soy Sauce: 2–3 tablespoons (adjust to taste).
  • Sesame Oil: 1 teaspoon (to finish).
  • Oyster Sauce: 1 tablespoon (optional, adds depth).
  • Butter: A small knob (optional, for extra richness like hibachi style).
  • Salt & White Pepper: To taste.

Steps

Step 1: Prep work

  1. Break up the Rice: Take your cold rice and break up any large clumps with your hands or a fork. This ensures each grain fries evenly.
  2. Whisk Eggs: Beat the eggs in a small bowl with a pinch of salt.
  3. Chop: Minced garlic, dice onions, and chop green onions.

Step 2: Scramble the Eggs

  1. Heat a wok or large pan over medium-high heat. Add a drizzle of oil.
  2. Pour in the eggs and scramble them quickly until just set.
  3. Remove the eggs from the pan and set them aside on a plate (so they don't overcook).

Step 3: Sauté Aromatics & Veggies

  1. Add a bit more oil to the hot pan.
  2. Add the onions and white parts of green onions. Stir-fry for 1 minute.
  3. Add the garlic and mixed vegetables. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes until the veggies are tender but crisp.

Step 4: Fry the Rice

  1. Turn the heat up to high.
  2. Add the cold rice and the knob of butter (if using).
  3. Stir-fry constantly for about 3 minutes. You want to toast the rice and heat it through. Tip: Press the rice into the pan to let it get a little crispy, then toss.

Step 5: Season & Combine

  1. Pour the soy sauce (and oyster sauce) evenly over the rice. Toss well to coat every grain in brown color.
  2. Add the cooked eggs back into the pan and break them up into smaller pieces as you mix.
  3. Season with white pepper and remove from heat.

Step 5: Finish

  1. Stir in the sesame oil.
  2. Garnish with the fresh green parts of the green onions.
  3. Serve immediately while piping hot!

Enjoy your meal!
